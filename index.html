The NEURON and NEST models associated with the paper:

Billeh YN, Cai B, Gratiy SL, Dai K, Iyer R, Gouwens NW, Abbasi-Asl R, Jia X, Siegle JH, Olsen SR, Koch C, Mihalas S, Arkhipov A (2020) Systematic Integration of Structural and Functional Data into Multi-scale Models of Mouse Primary Visual Cortex. Neuron 106:388-403.e18
doi: 10.1016/j.neuron.2020.01.040.

are available (all models, code, and meta-data) at the authors web portal

https://portal.brain-map.org/explore/models/mv1-all-layers

    Highlights:
  • Python 2.7 and 3.6 are supported.
  • model and simulation output are saved in SONATA format: https://github.com/AllenInstitute/sonata
  • "The in vivo extracellular recordings used for comparison are ... also freely available" https://portal.brain-map.org/explore/circuits/visual-coding-neuropixels

See the paper for documentation, tutorials, and references for above resources.
